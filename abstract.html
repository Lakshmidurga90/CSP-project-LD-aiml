Abstract

A survey is going to conduct how people are using different educational websites or apps in an area. For thissurvey we have selected Buchi Reddy Palam village, SPSR Nellore District so here the main theme is to know how people are using the educational websites and how they apply in real -time to get knowledge on the data/information. The main thing of the survey is to know how the people are uses different types of EDUCATIONAL WEBSITES like EDX, FUNBRAIN, DUOLINGO, COURSERA, PBS KIDS, KHAN ACADEMY, BRAINSCAPE, SCHOLASTIC ETC……, Also this survey helps to keep impact on how people utilize the education websites and how they used to get more knowledge using Educational Websites and how they utilize at the right situation. And the main disadvantage is lack of face-to-face interaction, potential for social isolation, limited ability to assess practical skills, increased risk of distractions, self discipline. By doing survey on the educational website, we can get more information on how people are using educational websites and also, we can improve the educational websites by using the disadvantages of how people are going to face issues using educational websites.For this community service project (CSP) we form a team to survey on the educational websites.
